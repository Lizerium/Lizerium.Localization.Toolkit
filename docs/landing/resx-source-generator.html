Strongly typed RESX source generator for .NET

Turn keys such as `MainWindow_Title` and `Settings_Log_FileCreated_Format` into discoverable C# methods.

var title = L.MainWindow.Title();
var message = L.Settings.Log.FileCreated(path);

The generator validates missing language keys and placeholder mismatches through diagnostics such as `LOC001` and `LOC002`.
